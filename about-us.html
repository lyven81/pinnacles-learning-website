Pinnacles Holding Limited

Company Overview

Pinnacles Holding Limited is committed to business innovation that bridges data intelligence, sustainable agriculture, digital commerce, and real estate. We strive to create enduring value through integrity, technology, and purposeful service.

Mission: Empower businesses and communities through insight, opportunity, and action.

Vision: To be a catalyst for sustainable and inclusive growth across Southeast Asia.

Core Values: Clarity, Innovation, Integrity, Sustainability, Collaboration

Our Story

Founded in 2017, Pinnacles Holding Limited began as a e-commerce retailer. Over the years, we expanded into property management, data consultancy, and agricultural ventures. Milestones include the launch of our first Airbnb unit in 2018, Shopee store in 2019, and the honeydew plantation initiative in 2022.
