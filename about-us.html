Our Story

Founded in 2017, Pinnacles Holding Limited began as a single-venture startup focused on e-commerce. Over the years, it has grown into a diversified holding entity with business verticals in e-commerce storefronts across Shopee, Lazada, and TikTok, short-term property leasing, honeydew plantation operations, and data consulting. With a commitment to responsible growth, we continue to innovate while maintaining deep respect for our communities and environment.
