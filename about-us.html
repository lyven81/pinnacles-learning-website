About Pinnacles Holding Limited

Mission: To empower diverse industries through data-driven decisions, sustainable operations, and innovative digital commerce.

Vision: To be a leading multi-sector holding company that creates value across technology, real estate, agriculture, and retail in Southeast Asia.

Values: Integrity, innovation, inclusiveness, sustainability, and measurable impact.

Our Story

Founded in 2017, Pinnacles Holding Limited began as a single-venture startup focused on data consulting. Over the years, it has grown into a diversified holding entity with business verticals in short-term property leasing, honeydew plantation operations, and e-commerce storefronts across Shopee, Lazada, and TikTok. With a commitment to responsible growth, we continue to innovate while maintaining deep respect for our communities and environment.
