About Pau Analytics

We help Malaysian SMEs turn data into clear direction. Our mission is to make analytics practical and genuinely useful—so your decisions aren't guesses, but confident steps forward. We take what you already have and turn it into insights you can act on immediately.

We deliver three core solutions, ready in 2-3 weeks. Analyst Reports assess your business health and uncover opportunities—identifying which customers will churn, which products drive profit, and where you're losing money. Dashboards display real-time sales, inventory levels, and customer trends at a glance—so you always know where your business stands. AI Assistant (24/7) monitors operations continuously and alerts you before problems happen—like detecting early churn signals or campaign performance drops.

Our analytics expertise covers six key areas that solve real business problems. Predictive Modeling identifies which customers will leave, which employees will quit, and which products will drive revenue. Segmentation Analysis finds your high-value customers, profitable product categories, and best-performing marketing channels.

Optimization Analysis improves campaign ROI, reduces cart abandonment, and maximizes profit margins. Time Series Analysis forecasts seasonal demand, predicts sales cycles, and plans inventory confidently. Regression Analysis reveals what drives customer spending, product returns, and business growth. Statistical Testing proves which marketing strategies work, which payment methods customers prefer, and which factors improve satisfaction.

We focus on solving problems that matter to you—keeping customers loyal, planning inventory smarter, understanding which campaigns work, and optimizing pricing. Every analysis is built from your perspective, whether you're managing operations, running marketing, or making strategic decisions. No confusing jargon, no overwhelming complexity.

Our reports and dashboards are designed to be practical and easy to understand. The goal isn't impressive tech talk or numbers on a screen—it's clarity that helps you act with confidence. You'll see measurable results, not complicated methodologies.

This is about using what you already have—your business data—to make better decisions and grow smartly. We work together to serve your customers better and drive real business growth. It's partnership, not just service delivery.

We're here to be your trusted partner in turning data into direction. Whether you're just starting with analytics or looking to get more value from existing data, we'll help you every step of the way. Let's make your data work for you.
