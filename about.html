About Us

We help Malaysian SME owners make sense of their business data. We work with owners and managers in retail, F&B, property, and services who have data sitting in spreadsheets — but no clear picture of what it means or what to do next.

Every project starts with a business question, not a dataset. We cover six types of analysis: predictive modelling, regression, statistical testing, segmentation, optimization, and time series — each tied to a decision you need to make.
