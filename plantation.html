Honeydew Plantation Operations

Our plantation division specializes in cultivating high-quality honeydew melons using sustainable agricultural practices. We integrate precision farming technologies and eco-friendly methods to ensure a consistent and nutritious yield while caring for the environment.

Focus Areas

  • Soil health monitoring and crop rotation
  • Greenhouse climate control and irrigation systems
  • Harvest planning and supply chain tracking
  • Farm-to-market distribution planning
  • R&D in organic farming and agri-tech innovation

Our Commitment

Led by our Head of Plantation Operations, we are committed to quality, sustainability, and transparency in every melon we grow and every market we serve.
