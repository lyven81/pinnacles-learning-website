Overview

Our honeydew plantation is located in Bukit Pelanduk, Negeri Sembilan, combining natural conditions with data-driven cultivation methods. We focus on sustainable practices that ensure both productivity and environmental stewardship.

What We Grow

  • Premium honeydew melons known for sweetness and texture
  • Grown in controlled environments for consistent quality
  • Harvested at peak ripeness and distributed directly to market

Our Approach

We use sensor-based monitoring, drip irrigation, and crop rotation to ensure plant health and efficient resource use. Our plantation team also explores natural pest control and organic fertilization strategies for long-term soil vitality.

Distribution & Partners

Our honeydew products are available through direct-to-consumer delivery and retail partners across the region. We welcome inquiries from grocers, hospitality chains, and export clients seeking premium produce with traceable origins.
