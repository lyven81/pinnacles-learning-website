Honeydew Melon Plantation | Bukit Pelanduk, Negeri Sembilan

Overview

Our plantation operations focus on cultivating premium-quality honeydew melons using sustainable farming practices. Located in Bukit Pelanduk, Negeri Sembilan, we believe in producing nutritious food while caring for the earth and uplifting local farming communities.

What We Grow

Honeydew Melon

Our signature crop is known for its sweetness, juicy texture, and consistent size. Grown on well-aerated soil under optimized sunlight, these melons yield a high volume with minimal waste. We are also exploring the expansion into related melon varieties to meet diverse market demands.

Sustainability & Innovation

We practice responsible agriculture through low-pesticide use and soil-enriching techniques. Drip irrigation helps minimize water waste while maintaining optimal hydration. Our operations incorporate smart sensors, weather tracking tools, and digital dashboards to monitor field conditions, crop health, and output forecasts. We also manage organic waste responsibly to reduce environmental impact.

Farm-to-Market Flow

Freshly harvested melons are cleaned, sorted, and packed daily for delivery to major wet markets across the country. Our logistics partners ensure that produce reaches shelves quickly, maintaining freshness and quality. In the future, we aim to integrate this flow with our e-commerce and B2B retail channels.
