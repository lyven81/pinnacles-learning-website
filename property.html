Short-Term Property Leasing

At Pinnacles Holding Limited, our property leasing division specializes in managing short-term stays for both business and leisure travelers. Whether through Airbnb, Booking.com, or direct arrangements, we create inviting spaces backed by professional service and flexible arrangements.

What We Offer

  • Curated homes and apartments in strategic urban and suburban locations
  • Fully furnished units with essential amenities and modern décor
  • Streamlined check-in and support process for guests
  • Dynamic pricing strategy to optimize occupancy and revenue
  • Maintenance, housekeeping, and guest review management

Partnership Opportunities

  • Full-service management to reduce owner workload
