Key Retention Insights

High Prediction Accuracy
Advanced machine learning model achieves 87% accuracy in predicting employee turnover risk 6 months in advance, enabling proactive intervention strategies.
Significant Cost Impact
Each employee departure costs approximately €45K in recruitment, training, and lost productivity, making retention investments highly cost-effective.
Critical Risk Periods
Employees show highest turnover risk during years 1-2 (adjustment period) and after 5+ years (career plateau), requiring targeted retention strategies.
Early Warning System
Predictive model identifies behavioral patterns and engagement signals that precede resignation decisions, creating intervention opportunities.

Strategic Retention Recommendations

Implement Early Warning System
Deploy the 87% accurate prediction model to identify at-risk employees 6 months early. Create automated alerts for HR teams to initiate retention conversations.
Targeted Retention Programs
Design specific interventions for high-risk periods: enhanced onboarding for 1-2 year employees and career development opportunities for 5+ year staff.
Cost-Benefit Analysis
Invest up to €20K per high-risk employee in retention strategies (44% of turnover cost) for positive ROI through reduced replacement expenses.
Continuous Model Improvement
Regularly update the prediction model with new data and feedback from retention interventions to maintain high accuracy and effectiveness.
