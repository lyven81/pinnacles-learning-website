Strategic Recommendations

Optimize Peak Hour Inventory
Schedule restocking 30-60 minutes before 2PM peak window to ensure full inventory during highest-demand periods. Monitor stock levels hourly during 2-4PM window to prevent stockouts.
Strategic Maintenance Timing
Schedule maintenance and cleaning during lowest-activity hours (typically early morning or late evening) to minimize revenue impact while maintaining optimal machine performance.
Location-Specific Expansion
Use €127 daily average as benchmark for new location evaluation. Focus expansion on locations with similar temporal patterns and demographic profiles for consistent performance.
Dynamic Pricing Strategy
Consider implementing premium pricing during peak hours (2-4PM) for high-demand items, while offering promotional pricing during slower periods to optimize overall revenue per machine.
