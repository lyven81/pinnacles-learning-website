Who We Are

Data Analytics Consulting for Malaysian SMEs

Pau Analytics helps Malaysian SME owners make sense of their business data. We work with owners and managers in retail, F&B, property, and services who have data sitting in spreadsheets — but no clear picture of what it means or what to do next.

We take your raw data and turn it into something you can actually use. That means a written case study that tells the business story, an interactive dashboard you can explore yourself, and — for complex decisions — an AI assistant that answers questions about your data in plain language.

We cover six categories of analysis: predictive modelling, regression, statistical testing, segmentation, optimization, and time series. Each project starts with a business question — not a dataset — so the output is always tied to a decision you need to make.

If you have data and a problem, we can help you understand what the data is saying and what to do about it.
