Data Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Data Privacy Policy explains how Pinnacles Holding Limited collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal and business data when you engage with our services, website, and analytics solutions.

2. Data We Collect

We collect data necessary to deliver our analytics services, including but not limited to:

  • Business and membership data (e.g., sales and customer records).
  • Personal details such as your name, email, and contact number when you submit forms or inquiries.
  • Technical data like IP addresses, browser type, and usage behavior on our website.

3. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to improve our services and deliver actionable analytics insights. Specifically, we use your data to:

  • Analyze and generate business insights based on membership or customer trends.
  • Communicate with you regarding your consultations, projects, or subscriptions.
  • Enhance our website functionality and user experience.
  • Comply with legal or regulatory obligations when required.

4. Data Protection & Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to ensure your data is secure. All data provided to us is treated confidentially and is only accessible by authorized personnel who require it to perform their duties.

5. Sharing of Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your data to third parties. Data may only be shared with trusted partners or service providers strictly for the purpose of fulfilling our services, under confidentiality agreements.

6. Data Retention

Your data will be retained only for as long as it is necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy or to comply with legal requirements.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to your personal data.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data when it is no longer required.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at director@pinnacleslearning.com.

8. Updates to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. The latest version will always be available on this page.
