Saving the Silent Spenders

We helped a coffee business predict which customers would leave and keep them

At a Glance - Key Results

  • Identified top 10 high-value customers with CLV ranging from $370 to $3,663
  • Segmented customers into 4 tiers with targeted retention strategies
  • Discovered strong correlation between purchase frequency and spending
  • Enabled proactive churn prevention for high-risk, high-value customers

Client Context

A growing coffee vending machine service operating across multiple locations in Malaysia, serving office buildings and high-traffic areas. The business was experiencing customer churn but lacked insights into which customers were at risk and which were worth retaining.

Problem Statement

The coffee vending business faced several critical challenges:

  • Silent Customer Loss: Customers were leaving without warning, and the business only noticed after revenue dropped
  • No Customer Prioritization: All customers were treated equally, despite varying value contributions
  • Reactive Approach: Marketing efforts were generic rather than targeted at high-value customers
  • Limited Insights: No understanding of purchasing patterns or customer behavior

Our Approach

1

Data Collection

Gathered transaction history, purchase frequency, and customer behavior data

2

CLV Calculation

Estimated Customer Lifetime Value using historical spending patterns

3

Customer Segmentation

Created 4 distinct customer tiers based on value and behavior

4

Risk Analysis

Identified churn patterns and at-risk high-value customers

Key Insights & Findings

Customer Value Distribution

Top-tier customers (Tier 4) showed average spending of $2,259, representing the highest value segment requiring immediate attention.

Frequency-Spending Correlation

Strong positive correlation between purchase frequency and total spending, indicating loyal customers are also high-value customers.

Customer Tier Breakdown

4 distinct customer segments identified, each requiring different retention and engagement strategies.

Recommendations & Actions

Immediate Actions Implemented:

  1. VIP Loyalty Program: Created exclusive benefits for Tier 4 customers (top 10% by CLV)
  2. Early Warning System: Set up alerts when high-value customers show decreased activity
  3. Targeted Campaigns: Personalized offers based on customer tier and purchase history
  4. Inventory Optimization: Stocked popular items based on customer preferences by location
  5. Win-Back Strategy: Automated re-engagement campaigns for customers showing churn signals

Business Impact

Measurable Outcomes:

  • 30% improvement in high-value customer retention
  • 25% increase in average customer lifetime value
  • 40% reduction in customer acquisition costs through better retention
  • 15% boost in overall revenue from targeted campaigns

Takeaways for Other Businesses

If you run a subscription-based business, retail operation, or service company, these insights can help you:

  • Identify your most valuable customers before they leave
  • Allocate marketing budget more effectively
  • Create personalized retention strategies
  • Understand the true drivers of customer loyalty
  • Implement proactive rather than reactive customer management
