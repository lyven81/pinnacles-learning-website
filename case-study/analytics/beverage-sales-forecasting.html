Seasonal Success

How time series forecasting helped a beverage retailer predict Chinese New Year demand and optimize inventory

At a Glance - Key Results

  • Reduced stockouts by 40% during peak seasonal periods
  • Decreased excess inventory by 25% through accurate demand prediction
  • Achieved 85% forecasting accuracy using ARIMA and seasonal models
  • Increased revenue by RM180,000 during Chinese New Year period

Client Context

A beverage distributor in Kota Kinabalu serving retail stores across Sabah. The business struggled with inventory planning for seasonal events, particularly Chinese New Year when demand for certain beverages spikes dramatically, leading to frequent stockouts and lost sales.

Problem Statement

The beverage distributor faced critical inventory challenges:

  • Unpredictable Demand: Massive spikes during Chinese New Year but unable to predict exact quantities needed
  • High Stockouts: Running out of popular drinks during peak demand periods
  • Excess Inventory: Over-ordering slow-moving products, tying up cash flow
  • Manual Planning: Relying on gut feeling rather than data-driven forecasting
  • Lost Revenue: Missed sales opportunities during high-profit seasonal periods

Our Approach

1

Historical Analysis

Analyzed 3 years of sales data to identify seasonal patterns and trends

2

Seasonal Modeling

Applied ARIMA and exponential smoothing models for demand forecasting

3

Validation Testing

Back-tested models against historical data to ensure accuracy

4

Implementation

Deployed forecasting system for real-time inventory planning
