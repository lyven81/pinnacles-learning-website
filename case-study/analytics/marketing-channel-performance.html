More Sales, Same Budget

How channel performance analysis helped an e-commerce business optimize marketing spend and increase ROI by 50%

At a Glance - Key Results

  • Identified Affiliate Marketing as top performer at 3.92 units sold per dollar
  • Achieved 78% accuracy in predicting sales outcomes
  • Increased overall marketing ROI by 50% through budget reallocation
  • Eliminated $25,000 in wasted advertising spend annually

Client Context

A mid-sized e-commerce retailer selling consumer electronics across Malaysia. The company was investing heavily in multiple advertising channels but struggled to understand which platforms delivered the best return on investment. With a monthly marketing budget of RM50,000, they needed data-driven insights to optimize spending.

Problem Statement

The e-commerce business faced critical marketing efficiency challenges:

  • Unclear ROI: No visibility into which advertising channels generated actual sales
  • Budget Misallocation: Equal spending across all channels without performance consideration
  • Wasted Spend: Continued investment in low-performing platforms
  • No Predictive Capability: Unable to forecast sales impact of budget changes
  • Decision Paralysis: Marketing team lacked confidence in budget allocation decisions

Our Approach

Data Integration

Collected performance data from 6 advertising channels over 12 months

2

Applied statistical modeling to understand spend-to-sales relationships

3

Calculated units sold per dollar for each channel

4

Predictive Modeling

Built forecasting model for budget optimization scenarios

Recommendations & Implementation

Strategic Actions Implemented:

  1. Budget Reallocation: Increased affiliate marketing spend by 60%, reduced TV and influencer budgets by 70%
  2. Performance Monitoring: Set up monthly tracking dashboard for ongoing optimization
  3. Testing Framework: Implemented A/B testing for social media and Google Ads improvements
  4. Predictive Planning: Used model for quarterly budget planning and forecasting
  5. Channel Optimization: Focused on improving underperforming but strategic channels

Business Impact

Measurable Outcomes (6 months post-implementation):

  • 50% increase in overall marketing ROI
  • RM25,000 annual savings from eliminating wasteful spend
  • 35% boost in sales volume with same budget
  • 78% accuracy in sales forecasting for planning
  • 40% reduction in customer acquisition cost

If you're running multi-channel marketing campaigns, this analysis approach can help you:
